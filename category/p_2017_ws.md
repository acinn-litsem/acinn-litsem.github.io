### Upcoming paper

On 23.01.2018 we will discuss this paper together:

- Forsythe, N., Fowler, H. J., Li, X.-F., Blenkinsop, S., & Pritchard, D. (2017) 
  Karakoram temperature and glacial melt driven by regional atmospheric circulation 
  variability. Nature Climate Change, 7(August). https://doi.org/10.1038/nclimate3361
  
There will be no preliminary presentation, though we will look at the paper figures on the beamer. Therefore can you all please prepare answers to the following questions to ensure we have a fruitful discussion:
- What is the most valuable finding of this paper?
- What are the wider implications of the study?
- What do you consider the best and worst aspects of the methodology used and why.

On 30.01.2018 Flo will present us with the final paper.
